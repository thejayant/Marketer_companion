Marketer Companion – Privacy Policy

Last updated: Oct 26, 2025

Summary: The extension connects to your Google account to read data from Google Search Console and Google Analytics (GA4) that you own or have access to. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties. All data stays in your browser and in Google services. You can disconnect anytime.

What data we access

  • Google Search Console: Verified site list, URL Inspection results, and Search Analytics aggregates (clicks, impressions, CTR, position).
  • Google Analytics (GA4): Aggregate metrics via the Analytics Data API and property/stream metadata via the Admin API.
  • Your email address: Shown inside the extension UI to indicate the signed-in account (via openid email scope).

How we use and store data

  • OAuth tokens are stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local. Tokens are used only to call Google APIs on your behalf.
  • No personal data is transmitted to external servers controlled by the developer.
  • You can sign out from the footer; this revokes local access and clears tokens.

Policy compliance

  • We request the minimum necessary scopes (webmasters.readonly/webmasters, analytics.readonly, openid email).
  • We comply with Google API Services User Data Policy and applicable product policies.

Contact

If you have questions or wish to request data deletion, contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store listing or email provided there.
